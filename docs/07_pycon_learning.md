# PyCon Learning

Submission prompt to answer:

> What aspects of the PyConSG26 programme have you learned from and applied?

## Current Application

- Hackathon Track 1 shaped the product direction toward career pathway exploration, skill-gap analysis, and learning action planning.
- The judging rubric shaped the repository structure. Process evidence lives in `docs/` and `logs/` from the start.
- Sponsor and partner tools influenced the architecture:
  - OpenAI for explanation and learning-plan generation.
  - Apify for market validation from job postings.
  - Exa for current learning resources and citations.
  - SkillsFuture datasets as the ground-truth data layer.

## Product Decisions Linked To The Programme

- The app uses SkillsFuture data first because the track rewards data integrity.
- The app records human-AI collaboration because the rubric assigns major weight to process.
- The recommendation flow separates scoring from explanation to support responsible AI use.
- The UI avoids a generic chatbot and answers a concrete career-transition question.
